About

Consisting of Jazz Vicaris, Syd Solanke, & Droogie

ABBATIA is a 3-piece rap group from Philly, with a hardcore hip-hop sound reminiscent of City Morgue, Death Grips, Ho99o9 but with pop-leaning tracks that have been described as “horror-core Outkast”.

The guys are lifelong best friends in their early 20s, bonded by a love for diverse genres of music, manga / anime, sci-fi, and creepypasta.

Production

100% of the music production and visuals are done in-house by Syd and Droogie (respectively). Droogie, who handles the visuals, has also done animation and live visuals for artists like Toro y Moi and Daedelus.

Recent Updates

Their next 6-song EP, Red Room, is out in September on A2B2 - a label recently launched by Andy Morin(Flatlander of Death Grips). ABBATIA is the label's 2nd signing. Andy discovered them in the A2B2 Discord community and quickly became addicted to bumping their tracks while driving through LA.

New EP

Standout tracks from the EP include the mosh-pit-inducing “Dreadnaught”; the creepy, face-melting “World Wide Web”, and the ghostly club pop track “System Failure”. These 3 tracks can be found here.

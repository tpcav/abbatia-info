ABBATIA

About

Consisting of Jazz Vicaris, Syd Solanke, & Droogie ABBATIA is a 3-piece rap group from Philly, with an industrial hip-hop sound reminiscent of City Morgue, Death Grips and Aphex Twin, but with a pop-leaning flavor that have been described as “horror-core Outkast”. These boys are lifelong best friends in their early 20s, bonded by a deep love for the niche worlds of music, manga, video games and film. The blood tinged vocals, along with Syd’s cutthroat digital drums and bass, create an onslaught of violent character on any given track.

100% of the music production and visuals are done in-house by Syd and Droogie (respectively). While Jazz forges the hooky structural mesh that makes these tracks as addictive as they are abrasive. Droogie has also done music videos and live visuals for artists like Toro y Moi and Daedelus. All of these respective skills construct a powerhouse for self-made media, that feels as if it’s coming from a monolithic cult.
